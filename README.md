## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm an ML Engineer who builds systems that **actually work in the real world** — not just on benchmarks. With a dual Master's background in **AI & Machine Learning** (University of Adelaide) and **Mechatronics Engineering** (Tor Vergata, Rome), I bridge the gap between intelligent algorithms and physical hardware.

I've shipped CV systems for infrastructure inspection, autonomous UAV navigation, and on-device inference on microcontrollers. When I deploy a model to a Jetson Nano or an STM32, I'm not guessing at the hardware limits — I've lived them.

- 🔭 Currently working on autonomous drone systems with ROS2, EGO-Planner & YOLO-based detection
- - 🌱 Exploring large-scale aerial CV with distributed inference pipelines (SAHI + Ray)
  - - 🧠 Passionate about edge AI, model compression, and making deep learning work on constrained hardware
    - - 🎯 97% on-device accuracy on STM32 microcontroller — no cloud, pure quantization magic
      - - ⚡ Fun fact: I once replaced a default feature tracker with SuperPoint and went from 5 features/frame to ~2,000!
        - - 📍 Based in Adelaide, Australia | Open to ML, CV, Robotics & Autonomous Systems roles
